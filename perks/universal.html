Class-Agnostic Perks

Perks available on all Exotic Class Items, regardless of Class.

Perk Breakdowns

Perk Column 1

Spirit of Inmost Light

Description

"Using an ability empowers the other two abilities, granting them improved energy regeneration."

Perk Breakdown

Upon using a Grenade or Class Ability, or upon dealing Powered Melee damage, one stack of “Empowered Abilities” is granted for 5 seconds, boosting the recharge rate of the two other abilities. Empowered Abilities can stack up to two times.

Empowered Abilities grants 400% / 800% bonus base Grenade and Melee regeneration rate and 25% / 50% bonus base Class Ability regeneration rate at 1 / 2 stacks.

While Empowered Abilities appears as only one buff on your screen, the recharge rate for your three Abilities is tracked separately. For example, if you use your Grenade ability, you will see “Empowered Abilities” on your screen, and your Melee and Class ability will gain the first tier of regeneration boost for five seconds. If you then use your Class Ability before the timer expires, your Grenade will gain the first tier of regeneration boost for the full duration, and your Melee will gain the second tier of regeneration boost for the full duration. Your Class Ability’s regeneration boost will not refresh and will expire after the initial five seconds has elapsed.

While any of your abilities have the second tier of regeneration boost, the buff on your screen will show as “Empowered Abilities x2.”

PvP Notes

While in any PvP mode, the regeneration boost for 1 / 2 stacks of Empowered Abilities is set to 100% / 200% for your Grenade and Melee Abilities. Class Ability regeneration scalar is unchanged.

Inherits

Spirit of Inmost Light inherits the improved Ability regeneration of the Titan Exotic chest armor Heart of Inmost Light.

It does not inherit the Ability damage buffs of Empowered Abilities, nor does it boost the durability of the Titan Barricade Class Abilities.

Interactions

  • Since Inmost Light activates on powered Melee damage, Abilities that inflict damage over time effects or hit multiple enemies will activate and maintain Empowered Abilities x2 for your Grenade and Class Abilities very easily. Some of these abilities include the Incinerator Snap on Warlock, the Threaded Spike on Hunter, and Shield Throw on Titan.

  • Warlock and Titan have access to unique Class Abilities Phoenix Dive and Thruster respectively. These Class Abilities have a notably lower base cooldown compared to others, allowing for much easier uptime on Empowered Abilities.

Spirit of the Assassin

Description

"Finishers and powered melee final blows grant invisibility."

Perk Breakdown

Upon obtaining a final blow using a powered Melee Ability or using your Finisher Ability on an enemy, you are granted the Void Invisibility buff for 7 / 10 seconds respectively, refreshing itself when you activate this effect again.

Void Invisibility is a typically Void-exclusive elemental buff that completely prevents enemies from being able to track your position while you are under its effects. However, performing any offensive action will prematurely end the Invisibility. If your only source of Invisibility is through Spirit of the Assassin, you will need to obtain another powered Melee or Finisher final blow to activate it again.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Assassin retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of the Assassin inherits the Void Invisibility portion of the Exotic Hunter helmet Assassin’s Cowl.

It does not inherit the health restore effect that would be supplied alongside the powered Melee and Finisher final blows.

Interactions

  • On Hunter, if you use your Grapple Grenade Ability immediately before entering Void Invisibility, it will not end the Invisibility effect until you activate your Grapple Melee. Because of this interaction, the Grapple Melee can be boosted by the Stylish Executioner Aspect’s Melee damage bonus upon exiting Invisibility, since Grapple Melee will break Invisibility.

  • The Warlock Lightning Surge and the Titan Consecration Aspects will both trigger the Invisibility effect, since they are considered Melee damage.

Spirit of the Ophidian

Description

"Weapons ready very quickly."

Perk Breakdown

Spirit of the Ophidian passively boosts all of your equipped weapons’ Handling stats by 35, allowing them to be readied and stowed extremely quickly. This boost is irrespective of weapon type, frame, element, or slot.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Assassin retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of the Ophidian inherits the full handling boost portion of the Exotic Warlock Gauntlets Ophidian Aspect.

It does not inherit the reload speed boost nor does it inherit the aerial effectiveness boost.

Interactions

  • While Spirit of the Ophidian does not feature any unique interactions due to the simplistic nature of its boost, some players may find it useful for quickly triggering swap-oriented effects, such as the Bait and Switch perk found on some Weapons.

Perk Column 2

Spirit of Synthoceps

Description

"Improves melee damage when you're surrounded."

Perk Breakdown

While you are within 15 in-game meters of at least 3 separate enemies, you gain the "Biotic Enhancements" buff for 5 seconds, refreshing as long as you are within the required range.

Biotic Enhancements grants a 165% increase to both powered and unpowered Melee damage, alongside a 100% increase to Glaive Melee damage.

PvP Notes

While in a PvP environment, the effectiveness of Biotic Enhancements is reduced to a 100% buff to powered and unpowered Melee damage. Glaive Melee damage is unchanged.

Inherits

Spirit of Synthoceps inherits the full Melee damage buff of the Exotic Titan Gauntlets Synthoceps.

It does not inherit the Super Ability damage boost from Biotic Enhancements, nor does it inherit the handling and reload speed boost while Biotic Enhancements is active.

Interactions

  • Biotic Enhancements will boost the damage of Solar Ignitions if they are caused by a Melee Ability. This includes Warlock’s Incinerator Snap Melee and Titan’s Consecration Aspect, causing them to output considerably high damage.

  • Since the Titan Consecration Aspect and Warlock Lightning Surge Aspect both inflict Melee damage, they are also boosted by Spirit of Synthoceps.

Spirit of the Star-Eater

Description

"While your Super energy is full, picking up an Orb of Power overcharges your Super, granting it bonus damage."

Perk Breakdown

While your Super Ability energy is fully charged, collecting an Orb of Power will grant one stack of "Feast of Light," which can stack up to a maximum of six times.

When you cast your Super Ability, all Feast of Light stacks will be consumed, granting your Super Ability an 18.2% / 36.4% / 54.6% / 59.7% / 64.8% / 70% boost to its damage at 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 / 6 stacks.

Feast of Light stacks have no maximum duration. They will persist until a Super Ability is cast or the player is defeated.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Star-Eater retains full functionality while in a PvP environment. However, the damage bonus against enemy players is redundant, as Super Abilities are already potent enough.

Inherits

Spirit of the Star-Eater inherits the Super Ability damage bonus from Feast of Light.

It does not inherit the bonus Super Ability energy gained from collecting Orbs of Power, nor does it inherit the healing or overshield effects when cast at full stacks of Feast of Light.

Interactions

  • The Ignitions caused by the Song of Flame Warlock Super do not gain the damage boost, which is inconsistent behavior and may be changed in the future.

  • The Needlestorm Warlock Super only receives the damage bonus on the initial impact of the projectiles and not the subsequent Threadling detonations, resulting in a lower overall benefit for this particular Super Ability.

Spirit of Verity

Description

"Weapon final blows with a damage type matching your grenade grant a stacking Grenade damage bonus."

Perk Breakdown

When you obtain an elemental weapon final blow that matches the element of your equipped grenade (i.e. a Solar weapon final blow while a Solar-element Grenade is equipped), you will be granted one stack of "Death Throes" for 10 seconds,, which can stack up to five times.

The first stack of Death Throes grants a 40% Grenade damage boost, with each subsequent stack adding an additional 15% Grenade damage boost. Therefore, at 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 stacks, Death Throes provides a 40% / 55% / 70% / 85% / 100% Grenade damage boost.

PvP Notes

Within a PvP environment, Death Throes instead provides a consistent 20% Grenade damage boost per stack.

Inherits

Spirit of Verity inherits the Grenade damage bonus of Death Throes from the Exotic Warlock Helmet Verity’s Brow.

It does not inherit the Grenade energy gained from elemental weapon final blows, nor does it inherit the boost to allies’ Grenade regeneration upon throwing a Grenade with Death Throes active.

Interactions

  • The Hunter Aspect Gunpowder Gamble is considered a Grenade ability and will inherit the damage boost from Death Throes. Although it is a Solar Ability, your elemental weapon final blows will need to match the element of your original Grenade to maintain Death Throes.

  • While Grapple Grenade is equipped on Hunter, the Grapple Melee will receive the damage boost of Death Throes.

  • The Prismatic Grenades for all three Classes also inherit the damage boost of Death Throes.

  • The unique Grenade Ability during Song of Flame receives the damage boost of Death Throes.
