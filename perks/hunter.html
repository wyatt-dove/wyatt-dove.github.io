Hunter-Specific Perks

Perks available only on the Hunter Exotic Class Item Relativism.

Perk Breakdowns

Perk Column 1

Spirit of the Dragon

Description

"Using your class ability reloads all weapons and increases weapon handling speeds for a brief time."

Perk Breakdown

Upon using any Class Ability (Marksman’s Dodge, Gambler’s Dodge, or Acrobat’s Dodge), "Wraithmetal Mail" is granted for 9 seconds. Upon gaining Wraithmetal Mail, all equipped weapons are automatically reloaded from reserves.

While Wraithmetal Mail is active, Handling and Reload Speed stats for all equipped weapons are set to their maximum value of 100, and a 0.7x multiplier is applied to all weapon ready/stow animations, allowing you to switch between weapons faster.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Dragon retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of the Dragon inherits the reload effect, the Handling and Reload Speed boosts, and the weapon ready/stow speed multiplier from the Exotic Hunter Chestplate "The Dragon's Shadow."

It does not inherit the boost of 50 to your character’s Mobility stat, the sprint speed boost, nor the slide distance boost.

Interactions

  • The reload effect of Wraithmetal Mail will activate weapon perks that require a reload, such as Voltshot.

  • Since Wraithmetal Mail reloads all equipped weapons, the use of Gambler’s Dodge or Acrobat’s Dodge is encouraged due to Marksman’s Dodge becoming irrelevant with this equipped.

Spirit of Galanor

Description

"Hits and final blows with your Super will return Super energy after it ends."

Perk Breakdown

Landing hits or final blows on enemies with your Super Ability will refund up to 50% of your Super Ability energy once your Super ends. The amount refunded will depend on how many hits and final blows alongside the Super Ability used.

PvP Notes

Spirit of Galanor retains full functionality while in a PvP environment. However, due to the power of Super Abilities in PvP environments alongside the generally low number of targets to hit, the expected Super energy refund will be much lower in practice.

Inherits

Spirit of Galanor inherits the Super Ability energy refund mechanic from the Exotic Hunter Gauntlets "Shards of Galanor."

It does not inherit the bonus Super energy gained from Throwing Knife final blows.

Interactions

  • The Silence and Squall Super Ability will consistently refund 50% of your Super energy as long as the tornado portion of the ability can reliably damage enemies.

  • The Silkstrike Super Ability will consistently refund 50% of your Super energy provided there are enough enemies to land final blows on or there is a Boss target that will survive multiple hits.

  • The Storm’s Edge Super Ability will consistently refund 50% of your Super energy if all three activations hit a boss target. Gains are lower if the Super is used to obtain final blows on weaker enemies.

  • The Shadowshot: Deadfall Super Ability will consistently refund 50% of your Super energy if enough enemies are both tethered to the anchor and defeated. Gains are lower if the enemies are not defeated or if the ability is used against a Boss target.

  • The Golden Gun Super ability is only able to refund up to 25% of your Super energy, even if all three shots are Precision Shots that land final blows.

Spirit of the Foetracer

Description

"Damaging a powerful combatant or Guardian with an ability grants you a temporary damage bonus for weapons matching that ability's element."

Perk Breakdown

Upon dealing any Ability damage to an enemy who is an Elite tier or higher, you are granted a x4 Weapon Surge for the element matching the ability (i.e. damaging a boss with a Void Smoke Bomb will grant a x4 Void Weapon Surge) lasting for 11 seconds. This buff cannot be refreshed or regained until the duration expires.

The x4 Weapon Surge effect is equivalent to a 25% boost to the corresponding elemental weapon damage.

PvP Notes

Within a PvP environment, the duration of the Weapon Surge effect is reduced to 6 seconds.

Inherits

Spirit of the Foetracer inherits the Weapon Surge effect from the Exotic Hunter Helmet "Foetracer."

It does not inherit the ability to highlight enemy targets hit by an ability, nor does it inherit the ability to spawn Elemental Subclass Pickups (Solar Firesprite, Void Breach, Arc Ionic Trace, Stasis Shard, or Strand Tangle) from defeating highlighted targets.

Interactions

  • Super Ability damage can also activate the Weapon Surge effect, making this a useful option when facing Boss opponents.

  • Gunpowder Gamble will grant the Solar Surge effect on hit.

Spirit of Caliban

Description

"Powered melee final blows trigger an ignition."

Perk Breakdown

Upon obtaining a powered Melee final blow using any Melee Ability, a Solar Ignition is created at the target’s location.

PvP Notes

Spirit of Caliban retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of Caliban inherits the ability to create Ignitions on powered Melee Ability kills from the Exotic Hunter Gauntlets "Caliban's Hand."

It does not inherit the boosts to Proximity Knife’s Scorch application nor the boost to recharge rate since Proximity Knife is not available on Prismatic Hunter.

Interactions

  • If using Grapple Grenade, the Grapple Melee will also trigger an Ignition.

  • Melee abilities that can reliably hit multiple targets are capable of triggering a chain of Ignitions if they land final blows. These abilities include Threaded Spike and Withering Blade.

  • Unlike with Spirit of Synthoceps, the Ignitions caused by Spirit of Caliban do not gain any benefits from melee-related buffs despite being caused by Melee Abilities. They do, however, gain benefits from other sources, such as the Prismatic Fragment "Facet of Ruin."

Spirit of Renewal

Description

"Allies inside the Duskfield grenade field gain Frost Armor and targets inside the field deal reduced damage."

Perk Breakdown

While enemy targets are within the radius of your Duskfield Grenade, they will suffer a 50% penalty to their outgoing damage dealt.

While you or any allies are within the radius of your Duskfield Grenade, one stack of Frost Armor is granted every 0.9 seconds.

Frost Armor is a Stasis-elemental buff that reduces incoming damage received by 4.5% per stack that you have. Prismatic Hunter may have up to 5 stacks of Frost Armor at maximum, for a total of 22.5% damage resistance. Frost Armor will last for 9 seconds, refreshing when you receive a new stack. If the timer expires, all stacks of Frost Armor are lost.

PvP Notes

Within a PvP environment, the outgoing damage penalty afflicted to enemy targets is reduced to 20%.

Inherits

Spirit of Renewal inherits the outgoing damage penalty to enemy targets as well as the Frost Armor application to yourself and allies from the Exotic Hunter Gauntlets "Renewal Grasps."

It does not inherit the boost to the radius of the Duskfield Grenade.

Interactions

  • Spirit of Renewal features no unique interactions.

Perk Column 2

Spirit of the Cyrtarachne

Description

"Gain Woven Mail when you use your grenade."

Perk Breakdown

Upon using a Grenade Ability, you are granted the Strand-elemental buff Woven Mail for 10 seconds.

While active, Woven Mail provides 45% damage resistance.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Cyrtarachne retains full functionality while in a PvP environment. However, the damage resistance provided specifically by the Woven Mail buff itself is reduced to 25%. Additionally, receiving precision hits or melee damage will bypass the damage resistance provided by Woven Mail.

Inherits

Spirit of the Cyrtarachne inherits the Woven Mail on Grenade Ability effect from the Exotic Hunter Helmet "Cyrtarachne's Facade."

It does not inherit the bonus to flinch resistance while Woven Mail is active.

Interactions

  • The Gunpowder Gamble Aspect is considered a Grenade Ability for Spirit of the Cyrtarachne, meaning you will gain Woven Mail each time you use the unique Grenade Ability.

  • Leveraging the quick cooldowns during Transcendence, it is relatively straightforward to maintain 100% uptime of Woven Mail while Transcendent since the Prismatic Grenade will also trigger this effect.

Spirit of the Gyrfalcon

Description

"Your Void weapons gain volatile rounds after you emerge from being invisible."

Perk Breakdown

When Void Invisibility applied to you expires by any means, you are granted the Volatile Rounds buff for 9 seconds. While Volatile Rounds is active, your Void-elemental weapons will inflict the Volatile debuff on hit.

Enemies afflicted with Volatile will suffer an additional small Void-elemental damage detonation after receiving sufficient damage while Volatile.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Gyrfalcon will only provide 3 seconds of Volatile rounds while in a PvP environment.

Inherits

Spirit of the Gyrfalcon inherits the Volatile Rounds on exiting Void Invisibility effect from the Exotic Hunter Chestplate "Gyrfalcon's Hauberk."

It does not inherit the unique Reserve Overshield effect when executing a Finisher Ability while under the effects of Void Invisibility.

Interactions

  • The Volatile Rounds buff, while active, will allow your Void-elemental weapons to pierce the shields of Barrier Champions.

Spirit of the Liar

Description

"Dealing damage with a powered melee or being hit by a melee attack will allow you to follow up with an extremely powerful Arc melee counterpunch."

Perk Breakdown

Upon receiving Melee damage or dealing powered Melee damage, you are granted the "Cross Counter" buff for 3 seconds. While Cross Counter is active, you receive a 200% boost to all Melee damage, and your Melee attack is replaced with a unique Melee attack that deals Arc-elemental damage. Cross Counter is consumed upon dealing Melee damage.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Liar retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of the Liar inherits the Cross Counter boost to Melee damage from the Exotic Hunter Gauntlets "Liar's Handshake."

It does not inherit the minor heal effect when hitting an enemy with the unique Cross Counter Melee attack.

Interactions

  • The Melee damage boost from the One-Two Punch Weapon perk is reduced to 40% while Cross Counter is active, aiming to prevent the Cross Counter Melee attack from doing obscene amounts of damage.

Spirit of the Wormhusk

Description

"Dodging gives a small health and shield bump."

Perk Breakdown

Upon using any of the Hunter’s Class Abilities (Marksman’s, Gambler’s, or Acrobat’s Dodge), gain a small instant heal effect equal to roughly 45 health.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Wormhusk retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of the Wormhusk retains the heal on Dodge activation effect from the Exotic Hunter Helmet "Wormhusk Crown." However, the amount of health gained from the effect is reduced from 67 to 45.

Interactions

  • As of Act 1 of Episode: Revenant, the heal effect does not activate when using the Ascension Aspect.

Spirit of the Coyote

Description

"Gain an additional class ability charge."

Perk Breakdown

Spirit of the Coyote grants a second Ability charge for your equipped Class Ability, which will begin recharging once your first Class Ability is fully charged.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Coyote retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of the Coyote inherits the additional Class Ability charge from the Exotic Hunter Chestplate "The Sixth Coyote."

It does not inherit the free copy of the Reaper armor mod, which creates an Orb of Power upon landing a final blow after casting a Class Ability.

Interactions

  • The additional Class Ability charge can be used with the Ascension and Threaded Specter Aspects, allowing for multiple uses of the unique Class Ability effects provided by those Aspects.
