Titan-Specific Perks

Perks available only on the Titan Exotic Class Item Stoicism.

Perk Breakdowns

Perk Column 1

Spirit of Severance

Description

"Powered melee or finisher final blows unleash a damaging explosion."

Perk Breakdown

Upon obtaining a powered Melee final blow or performing a Finisher, a Kinetic explosion is created at the target’s location, dealing up to 400 base damage. If this explosion obtains final blows, additional weaker explosions are created, dealing up to 268 base damage.

There is a 1 second cooldown between creating an explosion with a powered Melee or a Finisher, but the subsequent chain reaction explosions have no cooldown.

PvP Notes

The explosions created by Spirit of Severance suffer reduced damage output in a PvP environment.

Inherits

Spirit of Severance inherits the ability to create explosions upon obtaining powered Melee final blows or performing Finishers from the Exotic Titan Chestplate "Severance Enclosure."

It does not inherit the ability for these explosions to become more powerful by defeating higher-tier enemies.

Interactions

  • Consecration final blows will also trigger the explosions, since it is considered a Melee Ability.

Spirit of Hoarfrost

Description

"Your Barricade becomes a wall of Stasis crystals that slows nearby targets when created."

Perk Breakdown

When the wielder casts either a Rally Barricade or a Towering Barricade Class Ability, the Barricade is replaced with a wall of one small Stasis Crystal with two medium Stasis crystals on either side, mimicking the shape of a Rally Barricade. This Stasis Crystal Barricade will last for 14 seconds, and the Stasis Crystals will inflict the Stasis-elemental Slow debuff on nearby enemy targets when created.

While Spirit of Hoarfrost is equipped, the base cooldown of both Barricade Class Abilities is locked to the cooldown of Towering Barricade, which is 70 seconds. If the Thruster Class Ability is equipped, it will leave behind a pair of Stasis Crystals instead.

PvP Notes

Spirit of Hoarfrost retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of Hoarfrost inherits the Stasis Crystal Barricade effect from the Exotic Titan Chestplate "Hoarfrost-Z."

It does not inherit the Weapon Stability, Reload Speed, Range, and Flinch Resistance bonuses of a Rally Barricade.

Interactions

  • The Stasis Crystals created by Spirit of Hoarfrost are affected by Shatter damage buffs, such as the Prismatic Fragment Facet of Ruin.

  • As of Act 1 of Episode: Revenant, the Thruster Class Ability will leave behind a pair of Stasis Crystals on use with this perk equipped.

Spirit of the Eternal Warrior

Description

"Gain bonus damage for weapons matching your Super's element when your Super ends."

Perk Breakdown

When the wielder’s Super Ability expires, a x4 elemental Weapon Surge will be granted for 30 seconds, with an element that matches the equipped Super Ability’s element (i.e. Casting the Void Super Ability Twilight Arsenal will grant a x4 Void Weapon Surge when the Super ends).

The x4 Weapon Surge is equivalent to a 25% boost to Weapon damage.

PvP Notes

The Weapon Surge duration is reduced to 6 seconds while in a PvP environment.

Inherits

Spirit of the Eternal Warrior inherits the elemental Weapon Surge effect after your Super Ability expires from the Exotic Titan Helmet "Eternal Warrior."

It does not inherit the ability to gain Weapon Surge stacks from obtaining elemental Weapon final blows, nor does it inherit the overshield effect while in a Super Ability.

Interactions

  • If elemental Weapon Surge Armor Mods are equipped, the x4 Weapon Surge effect will temporarily overwrite them, granting the highest tier of Surge damage bonus of 25%.

Spirit of the Abeyant

Description

"Improves Drengr's Lash. Drengr's Lash projectiles track targets more aggressively and travel farther."

Perk Breakdown

While the Drengr’s Lash Aspect is equipped, upon casting any Barricade Class Ability, two additional Lashes are created, which create a fan-like formation. Lashes travel further and can seek enemy targets more aggressively. Lashes will inflict the Strand-elemental debuff Suspend on all targets they come into contact with. If the Thruster Class Ability is equipped, it instead creates a seeking Strand Tangle at the wielder’s position, which tracks to nearby enemy targets and creates a detonation that inflicts Suspend on all affected targets.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Abeyant retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Interactions

  • Spirit of the Abeyant will have no effect if the Drengr’s Lash Aspect is not equipped.

  • When Thruster is equipped, the seeking Strand Tangle is identical in functionality with the Tangles created by Warlocks with the "The Wanderer" Aspect equipped.

Spirit of the Bear

Description

"Move faster while guarding with the Unbreakable shield. Damage blocked with Unbreakable is converted to Super energy."

Perk Breakdown

While the wielder is blocking damage using the Unbreakable Aspect, the movement penalty incurred is significantly reduced, and Super Ability energy will be granted when Unbreakable is released depending on how much damage was blocked, up to a maximum of 20% Super energy per use of Unbreakable.

PvP Notes

The Super energy gains from Unbreakable are capped at 10% per use of Unbreakable while in a PvP environment.

Inherits

Spirit of the Bear inherits the movement speed increase and Super energy gains from the Exotic Titan Gauntlets "Ursa Furiosa."

It does not inherit the movement speed increase and Super energy refund from Sentinel Shield, since Sentinel Shield is not accessible on Prismatic Titan.

Interactions

  • Spirit of the Bear grants a flat amount of Super energy with its effect, irrespective of differing Super Ability cooldowns. This can allow for high uptime on Super Abilities with longer cooldowns.

  • With the changes that Unbreakable received in Episode 2: Revenant, the maximum Super energy gains can be more reliably reached compared to before, making Spirit of the Bear much more consistent.

Perk Column 2

Spirit of Contact

Description

"Damaging a target with a powered melee causes all nearby targets to suffer lightning strikes and become jolted."

Perk Breakdown

Upon dealing powered Melee damage to an enemy target, all enemies near the target are struck with lightning, dealing 200 base Arc-elemental damage and inflicting the Arc-elemental Jolt debuff.

Jolted targets will chain additional Arc damage to nearby targets when they receive damage.

PvP Notes

The lightning strikes created by Spirit of Contact have their base damage reduced to 50 while in a PvP environment.

Inherits

Spirit of Contact inherits the Jolting lightning strike effect on powered Melee damage from the Exotic Titan Gauntlets "Point-Contact Cannon Brace."

It does not inherit the Melee Ability energy refund on powered Melee final blows, nor does it inherit the increased lightning strike damage while under the effects of the Arc-elemental buff Amplified.

Interactions

  • The Shield Throw melee ability can be used to inflict the Jolting lightning strikes from long ranges.

  • The Consecration Aspect can inflict the Jolting lightning strikes with the first wave of the attack, allowing the wielder to expend only half of a Melee charge for the Jolt effect.

Spirit of Scars

Description

"Final blows with weapons that have a damage type matching your Super's element create a burst of healing around you that grants allies restoration."

Perk Breakdown

When the wielder obtains elemental Weapon final blows that match the element of the equipped Super Ability (i.e. a Void-elemental Weapon final blow with the Void Super Twilight Arsenal equipped), a burst of healing is created centered around the wielder. This burst of healing grants the Solar-elemental buff Restoration to the wielder and all allies within 15 meters for 3 seconds. There is a 3 second cooldown between activations of this healing burst.

While Restoration is active, health will consistently regenerate at a rate of 35 health per second, and this regeneration will not be halted by incoming damage.

PvP Notes

Spirit of Scars retains full functionality while in PvP environments. However, the effectiveness of the Solar-elemental buff Restoration specifically is reduced to 17.5 health granted per second.

Inherits

Spirit of Scars inherits the ability to gain Restoration upon elemental Weapon final blows from the Exotic Titan Helmet "Precious Scars."

It does not inherit the overshield aura from reviving or being revived by an ally.

Interactions

  • Spirit of Scars does not feature any unique interactions. However, it is recommended to lean heavily into matching-elemental interactions if using this perk, such as incorporating Harmonic mods into your armor.

Spirit of the Horn

Description

"Your class ability unleashes a blast of Solar energy that scorches targets."

Perk Breakdown

Upon casting any Barricade Class Ability, a Solar wave is launched directly forward from the Barricade that travels 18.5 meters before returning to the Barricade. This Solar wave inflicts 480 base Solar-elemental damage and inflicts 60 stacks of the Solar-elemental debuff Scorch. The Solar wave can damage enemies once on its initial cast and once while it is returning to the Barricade.

If Thruster is equipped, a Solar ball will be left behind on cast that explodes after a brief delay, inflicting the Scorch effect on nearby targets.

PvP Notes

The damage inflicted by the Solar wave is reduced to 100 base damage, and the stacks of Scorch inflicted are reduced to 30 while in PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of the Horn inherits the Solar wave on Barricade effect from the Exotic Titan Helmet "Kephri's Horn."

It does not inherit the bonus Class Ability energy gained from Solar-elemental final blows.

Interactions

  • If the Solar wave damages an enemy target twice while not in a PvP environment, it will reach the Scorch stack threshold for causing a Solar Ignition.

  • As of Act 1 of Episode 2: Revenant, functionality with Thruster was added. Its effectiveness is still undergoing testing.

Spirit of Alpha Lupi

Description

"Generates a healing pulse when your class ability is activated."

Perk Breakdown

Upon casting any Barricade Class Ability, the wielder and any allies within a 12 in-game meter radius will instantly be healed by 80 Health and 75 Shields. This effect requires direct line-of-sight between the wielder and allies.

If Thruster is equipped, the healing pulse will be generated on cast.

PvP Notes

Spirit of Alpha Lupi retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of Alpha Lupi inherits the healing effect for the wielder and nearby allies from the Exotic Titan Chestplate "Crest of Alpha Lupi."

It does not inherit the ability for Super Abilities to create extra Orbs of Power.

Interactions

  • As of Act 1 of Episode 2: Revenant, functionality with Thruster was added. Its effectiveness is still undergoing testing.

Spirit of the Armamentarium

Description

"Gain an additional grenade charge."

Perk Breakdown

The wielder receives one extra Grenade Ability charge, which will begin charging once the initial Grenade Ability is fully charged.

PvP Notes

Spirit of the Armamentarium retains full functionality within PvP environments.

Inherits

Spirit of the Armamentarium inherits the additional Grenade Ability charge from the Exotic Titan Chestplate "Armamentarium."

It does not inherit the free copy of the Armor Mod "Firepower," which creates an Orb of Power upon obtaining Grenade Ability final blows.

Interactions

  • If the wielder is using the Unbreakable Aspect in conjunction with Spirit of the Armamentarium, careful monitoring of Grenade energy usage is necessary. If Unbreakable is released before the extra Grenade charge is fully expended, then Unbreakable will only consume the extra Grenade charge. However, if Unbreakable is held long enough to begin draining the initial Grenade charge, then Unbreakable will fully consume both the extra Grenade charge and the initial charge.

  • Spirit of the Armamentarium does not grant an additional Prismatic Grenade charge while the wielder is Transcendent. Transcendence will, however, fully refund both normal Grenade charges when it expires.
